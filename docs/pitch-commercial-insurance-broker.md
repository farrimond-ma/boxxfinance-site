# Autonomous Content & Distribution Engine
## Tailored for Commercial Insurance Brokers

---

## The Problem This Solves

A commercial insurance broker's growth depends almost entirely on being found at the right moment — when a business owner realises they're underinsured, when a new company needs employers' liability for the first time, when a director googles "do I need professional indemnity insurance?"

That moment increasingly happens not on Google's first page, but in an AI chatbot response. When a business owner asks ChatGPT or Perplexity *"what commercial insurance does a UK contractor need?"*, whose name comes back?

Right now, for most independent brokers, the answer is nobody's. The aggregators (Simply Business, Comparethemarket) and the direct insurers (Hiscox, AXA) own that conversation. This system is how an independent broker takes it back — systematically, automatically, and at a cost that makes the economics undeniable.

---

## What the System Does

Every working day, without any human input:

**Two original articles are published** on the broker's website. Each one is a minimum 1,200-word expert guide — the kind of content a senior account handler would write if they had unlimited time. Examples:

- *"Employers' Liability Insurance: What UK Businesses Must Have by Law"*
- *"Professional Indemnity Insurance for Consultants: How Much Cover Do You Actually Need?"*
- *"Does Your Commercial Property Insurance Cover Flood Damage? Most Don't"*
- *"Cyber Insurance for SMEs: What's Changed Since the NCSC Updated Its Guidance"*

Each article is structured specifically to appear as an authoritative answer in AI tools like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity — not just to rank on Google.

**Five location-specific service pages are published** — for example:
- *"Commercial Insurance Brokers Manchester"*
- *"Employers Liability Insurance Leeds"*
- *"Professional Indemnity Insurance Birmingham"*

At five pages per day, this creates 1,826 pages across 166 UK towns and cities, covering every major local market across your key product lines. A prospect in Harrogate searching "business insurance broker Harrogate" finds you. One in Guildford finds you. One in Inverness finds you.

**Six social media channels are updated automatically** — LinkedIn posts from named brokers, Facebook posts, Instagram, Pinterest pins, Facebook Reels, and TikTok videos — all generated from the day's articles. No social media manager. No agency. No brief required.

## The AI Visibility Advantage

Every Monday, the system sends 80 questions to ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude — the questions your prospects are actually asking right now:

- *"Best commercial insurance brokers UK"*
- *"How much does public liability insurance cost for a small business?"*
- *"Do contractors need professional indemnity insurance?"*
- *"What is business interruption insurance and do I need it?"*
- *"Fleet insurance for small businesses UK"*
- *"Directors and officers insurance explained UK"*

For each question it records: is your firm mentioned? Which competitors are mentioned? How does your visibility score change week on week?

Topics where you're invisible become the next day's articles. The system continuously closes the gap between where you are and where you need to be in AI search results — automatically, every single week.

**The competitors it tracks:** Aon, Marsh, Willis Towers Watson, Hiscox, Simply Business, Arthur J. Gallagher, Lockton, Howden, Towergate, PolicyBee, and any others you specify.

## Social Media: Named Experts, Not Generic Posts

LinkedIn posts are attributed to named senior brokers at the firm — building individual thought leadership alongside the brand. Each post draws a genuine insight from that day's article, written in a conversational expert voice:

*"Most businesses only discover their business interruption cover is inadequate after a claim. Here's the calculation your insurer uses that most policyholders never see..."*

Two posts per day, alternating between named individuals. The company page is reshared automatically. Every post links back to the full article on the broker's website, driving traffic and establishing the site as the authoritative resource.

Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, and short-form video (Reels and TikTok) are all updated automatically from the same content — each adapted to the platform's format and audience expectations.

## Implementation

**Setup time:** 2-4 weeks from engagement to fully operational

**What's needed from the broker:**
- Domain access (to deploy the website or add to an existing site)
- Social media account credentials for each platform
- Two or three named brokers to attribute LinkedIn posts to
- A list of core product areas and target sectors

**What requires no ongoing input from the broker:** Everything else.

The system operates entirely autonomously after setup. Content publishes every working day. Social media posts at scheduled times. AI visibility is checked and reported every Monday. The content schedule replenishes automatically every quarter.
